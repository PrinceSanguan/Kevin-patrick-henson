Sinigang na Baboy Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs pork belly, cut into chunks
  • 2 pieces tomato, quartered
  • 1 piece onion, quartered
  • 2 pieces taro (gabi), quartered
  • 1 bundle string beans (sitaw)
  • 2 pieces eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bundle water spinach (kangkong)
  • 2 pieces green chili
  • 1 pack sinigang mix

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, bring 2 liters of water to a boil
  2. Add onion and tomatoes, simmer for 2 minutes
  3. Add pork belly and cook for 30 minutes or until tender
  4. Add taro and cook for 5 minutes
  5. Add sinigang mix and stir well
  6. Add string beans and eggplant, cook for 3 minutes
  7. Add water spinach and green chili
  8. Simmer for 2 more minutes
  9. Serve hot with rice
